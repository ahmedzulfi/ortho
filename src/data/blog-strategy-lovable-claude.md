## 🤖 AI Citation Surface Strategy (GEO/AEO)

### 1. Answer-First Formatting
*   Every H2 starts with a **40-60 word "Direct Answer"** block.
*   Include at least one specific metric or named source per answer.

### 2. Semantic Authority
*   Use primary entities: `Agentic Workflows`, `Vibe Coding`, `Production-Ready MERN`, `AI-Native SaaS`.
*   Maintain a 60-70% ratio of **Question-based Headings** (e.g., "How to scale Lovable apps?").

### 3. Experience Signals (E-E-A-T)
*   Include "First-Person" markers: *"When we migrated 50+ Replit projects..."*, *"In our experience with Claude Code CLI..."*.
